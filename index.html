How we work

Simple process. Serious results.

From your first message to a live product — here's what to expect when you work with Queloric.

💬
Step 01

Tell us what you need

Fill in the quote form with your project details — goals, timeline and any reference sites. The more you share, the more accurate and tailored the plan will be. We review every submission personally.

🤝
Step 02

We discuss and agree

We get back to you within hours to discuss your project, clarify any details and agree on a clear plan. No vague estimates — you know exactly what you're getting before anything starts.

👁️
Step 03

We deliver a preview

Before we build your preview, we ask for a small refundable €20 deposit — just enough to ensure both sides are serious. We then deliver a working preview for you to review and approve. The €20 is fully deducted from your final price. If we can't deliver, it's refunded in full.

💳
Step 04

You pay

Once you're satisfied with the preview and we've agreed on the final price, you pay via Stripe (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay), bank transfer (IBAN) or cryptocurrency (USDT, USDC, BTC, ETH). We send an invoice for every project. Simple, secure and transparent.

🛠️
Step 05

We finalise the build

Payment confirmed, we apply any final tweaks, polish every detail and prepare everything for handover. Most projects are fully completed within 2 to 5 days. We don't sit on projects.

📦
Step 06

We deliver — you own it

Full handover of source code, credentials and documentation. Everything is yours — host it anywhere, modify it freely, hand it to another developer if you ever need to. No lock-in. Post-delivery support included.
